📖 How to Play

1. Tap START — the rocket moves from 0 to 11s.

2. When the rocket hits a target (2s, 4s, 6s, 8s, 10s), a 1-second window opens.

3. Tap the big circle button within that window!

4. If you miss the 1s window, it counts as a miss and moves to the next target automatically.

🎯 Goal: Minimize your average reaction difference. <0.3s = Pro!
